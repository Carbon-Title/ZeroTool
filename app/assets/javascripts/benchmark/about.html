Maalka and Architecture 2030 have developed the Energy Target and Rating Tool for building owners and 2030 members to effectively compare their energy-use intensity (EUI) with similar building types. This helps to understand a building’s EUI efficiency change over time, and to set targets for achieving desired performance scores based on 2003 CBECS data. Basing the tool functionality on the 2003 CBECS dataset enables 2030 members to maintain pre-existing goals and baselines so that progress can continue to be consistently measured.
