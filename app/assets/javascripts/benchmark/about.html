Maalka removes barriers to dynamic data-driven sustainability initiatives with an open, secure platform for managing data, teams and programs that span thousands of buildings. Built for scalability, flexibility and security, Maalka integrates public and private data with analytics and an open-API to enable anyone to build and deploy custom applications quickly and economically.
