COMPUTE BULK METRICS FROM YOUR CSV FILE

  1. Click 'Select CSV' Button at the Bottom and Navigate to the CSV that you want to Upload
    1. Make sure that your CSV entries are formatted properly (see below)
    2. Make sure that the following building types are named as such:

      "DataCenter"
      "Hospital"
      "Hotel"
      "K12School"
      "MedicalOffice"
      "MultiFamily"
      "Office"
      "FinancialOffice"
      "Parking"
      "ResidenceHall"
      "Retail"
      "SeniorCare"
      "Supermarket"
      "Warehouse"
      "RefrigeratedWarehouse"
      "WastewaterCenter"
      "WorshipCenter"

  2. Click Submit
  3. Two CSV Files will be Downloaded Automatically
    1. Results of all successfully processed entries will be downloaded to 'processed.csv'
    2. All unprocessed entries (errors) will be downloaded as 'unprocessed.csv'
